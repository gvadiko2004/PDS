FAQ

What range of services do you offer

We offer a wide range of design and construction support services, including developing design projects, creating visualizations, providing author supervision, project management, and full implementation. Our services encompass both residential and commercial properties.

What makes the process of working with you stand out?

Our process includes several stages, from the initial consultation and discussion of your concept creation wishes to visualization, preparation for implementation, and project management. Each stage is designed to maximize comfort for our customers.

How long does a project take?

The time it takes to complete a project depends on its scope and complexity. It can usually take anywhere from a few weeks to several months. We always provide our clients with an estimated timeline at the beginning of the project.

Is it possible to make adjustments to the project while it's in progress?

Yes, we are open to making changes at any stage of the work. Please discuss all changes with our project manager to assess their impact on timelines and budget.

How do you craft the project budget to ensure success?

The project budget is determined based on your preferences, the size of the site, and chosen materials. We always strive to provide optimal solutions that ensure quality and functionality while fitting your budget.

Do you collaborate with contractors of your choice, or can I choose my own?

We have established relationships with reliable contractors to ensure high- quality work. However, if you prefer to work with your own contractor, we are open to considering collaboration with them.

What materials do you use in your projects?

We only use high - quality materials that meet international standards. Our experts can advise you on the best options for your project, considering your aesthetics and functionality requirements.

Do you offer design supervision during the project implementation process?

We offer project design supervision as an additional service to ensure strict implementation of the approved design visualization. Our specialists continuously monitor the process and are ready to resolve any issues during the design project implementation.

What is your policy regarding design changes after they have been approved?

If you decide to make changes after design approval, we're ready to consider your wishes. However, changes can affect the timeline and budget, so it is important to discuss all the details with our project manager.

Is there a warranty for the work that has been done?

Yes, we offer a guarantee for all materials used in our designs. Our goal is to ensure complete customer satisfaction and address any concerns after the project is completed.
